P3 : Ideate, Lo-Fi and Test

Brainstorming possible solutions and user testing through Lo-Fi prototypes.

Process

Drawing insights from user research, we brainstormed design ideas and possible solutions to the user needs. We then defined criteria to narrow down our ideas, and created storyboards of emergent ideas to show the entire scope of interaction. Following this, we designed and tested a Lo-Fi prototype amongst prospective users to gather some insights. Finally, we conducted market research to identify similar products in our problem space and outlined the value add of our unique solution.

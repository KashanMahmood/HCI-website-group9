P6 : Final Presentation

Creating a pitch and presenting our app in the Technigala Showcase.

Process

Through these milestones we completed the full user-centered design process for our project. We started by understanding and emphasizing with a well-defined user group, finding open problems, ideating solutions, prototyping, and then testing. Now that we have a functioning prototype, as the final milestone, we presented our project to our class and in Technigala to gather feedback and comments.
